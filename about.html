ABOUT | MOT Pitch Programme

Background

The Mosi-Oa-Tunya Pitch program under Jacaranda Hub was created in collaboration with AfriLabs and GoDoHub. The program is an investor readiness and financing platform that will help rising entrepreneurs gain the traction and resources they need to launch their product or service. The program is targeted at entrepreneurs who have developed an idea, service or product that is ready to launch. It is particularly aimed at entrepreneurs struggling to gain investment for their business to take off. The MOT Pitch aims to:

  • 1. Offer a community that provides training, mentorship, funding and visibility to over 100 Zambian startups over a 24-month period.

  • 2. Develop a network with over 200 mentors from Zambia, Nigeria and other countries.

  • 3. Provide access to local and global investors in order to grow their appetite for Zambia as an investment destination.

Problem Statement

28% of Zambia’s booming population is between 15-29. However, Zambia’s youth also have the highest rate of unemployment when compared to other age groups, peaking at a staggering 45%. Youth unemployment has been steadily increasing over the past decade or so, from 16% in 2012, finding a stable but disappointing plateau of 22% in 2020.

The ILO attributes the high rates of unemployment among young Zambians to insufficient rates of quality education, skills, or entrepreneurship.

The UN recognises that with the right support, Zambia’s young population can significantly impact Zambia’s economy, society, and Zambia’s progression towards the UN’s 2030 Vision of transitioning from a low to middle income country.

PROJECT OBJECTIVES AND EXPECTED RESULTS

OBJECTIVE RESULT
Provide ENGAGING content on investor-readiness and the Perfect Pitch. Empowered young individuals who feel equipped to deliver their Perfect Pitch with confidence and expertise.
Empowered young individuals who feel equipped to deliver their Perfect Pitch with confidence and expertise. Motivated and self-sufficient young people who can seek collaboration and quality advice from mentors, investors and each other.
Encourage young people to think outside-the-box to INNOVATE creative entrepreneurial solutions for the Zambian economy. Long-term benefit for the nation’s development by injecting reliable ambassadors of fresh and relevant products into the economy.

PROJECT ACTIVITIES

Mentorship Program

Connects young people with industry leaders to give them valuable insight, advice and inspiration.

TV Show

Offers critical exposure of candidates’ pitching strengths, their story and their businesses.

Training Program

Provides candidates with vital feedback on their business, brand, and education on how to attract investors.

Dealbook

A document with candidates’ profiles and businesses, opening them to the investor space for candidates so that even those who are unsuccessful on MOT can still access the investor community.

Virtual Deal Room

An app that integrates with social media and allows potential investors to easily access candidates’ profiles and communicate with them about their business
